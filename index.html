DAVID — Diversity, Awareness, Virtual, Inclusion Device

Our goal is to raise awareness of diverse perspectives students have on food, encourage empathy and curiosity, and transform mealtime into moments of reflection and cultural connection.

View Source Try It / Demo Read Docs
  • University of Queensland · DECO3500

What You Can Do

Capture Stories

Press a button, snap a photo, and record a short story about your meal. Transcripts make every voice accessible.

Listen & Read

Browse a shared board of colorful notes. Play audio or skim transcripts to explore diverse perspectives.

Reflect & Connect

Prompts encourage empathy, curiosity, and conversation—turning mealtime into moments of cultural connection.

On‑Device by Design

Runs on Raspberry Pi with local transcription (whisper.cpp). Prototype friendly and deployable in shared spaces.

Try the Prototype

Local Demo (Dev)

  1. Install deps: ffmpeg, Python 3.10+, Node 18+.
  2. In the repo, run DAVID/dev-setup-windowed.sh (or DAVID/board/start.sh for the UI + API).
  3. Open the board at http://localhost:3000.

For Educators

Deploy a supervised prototype in a dining space to spark conversations and reflection. See our Wiki for study guidance and ethics tips.
